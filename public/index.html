Welcome to the home of AFC Roza!




Founded in 2022, AFC Roza are currently competing in our second ever season in the Barnet Sunday Football League. Our first season saw us finish second in Division 6 and, after a league reshuffle, we are now battling our way through Division 1.

We are a fully licensed and affiliated football club, sustainably building club infrastructure off the pitch whilst striving to grow and improve on it.


If you want to find any information about the club, you have come to the right place. On this website you can find the latest news about the club, our upcoming opponents, current squad and much more. You can also buy any of the official club merchandise through the club shop.
